WELCOME TO KOTTAYAM

Kottayam is a city in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India. It is the district headquarters of the district and is located about 151 km north of the state capital Thiruvananthapuram.Illikkal Kallu is a monolith located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India. The distance from kottayam railway station to illikal kallu is 57km. Situated at around 3500 feet above sea level, Illickal Kallu is a major tourist attraction in Moonnilavu Gramapanchayat.
